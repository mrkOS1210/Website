About Us

A revamped 1950s milk bar complete with curvy glass counter, retro fit-out and old-fashioned service, it's a favourite hangout for everyone from harried commuters to loved-up teens and nostalgic nannas. Long considered an institution among Caringbah natives, it has commendably good coffee, exquisite hand-made chocolates and a range of imported American, British and European sweets. Park yourself on a candy-coloured bench seat and order a piccolo caffe latte full of body with an appropriately chocolate-like finish.

Our History

Parry opened his first milkbar in the 1940s, near Kogarah.

Over the next two decades, Parry opened more milkbars, Including the one near Caringbah Station.

By 1969, Parry's Caringbah was the only one to survive.

Parry sold the Caringbah milkbar in 1969 to a pair of Greek brothers.
